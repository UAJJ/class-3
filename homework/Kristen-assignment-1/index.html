The Paragraphs Were Wrongly Imprisoned

These were tricks performed using Javascript. Yes, Javascript. No slight of the hand or trick of the eye, no sawing lovely ladies in fanciful costumes in half, no doves popping out of hats. One day they took their Javascript magic too far. They made a woman disappear on-click using Javascript. But that wasn't all that disappeared. Next thing you know a man disappears and so does a small parrot. They were wrongly accused of kidnapping, found guilty and sent to a div jail.
